Our Story

14 Years of Engineering Excellence

Mavex Engineering is a specialized supplier and engineering partner focused on chemical systems for industrial process facilities.

With 14 years of engineering experience, including 8 years dedicated specifically to chemical systems, Mavex brings strong technical depth backed by real construction and field experience. Our approach is practical, thorough, and focused on delivering designs that are efficient, buildable, and aligned with real project needs.

In addition to design support, Mavex helps clients with pricing and sourcing chemical system parts, tubing, piping, and specialty components for industrial applications.

Mavex works with industrial clients, design-build teams, owners' representatives, and contractors across the U.S. and international markets.
