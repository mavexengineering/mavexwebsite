What We Do

Our Core Services

We work with owners' representatives, design-build teams, and industrial contractors who need efficient, constructable solutions for chemical process systems. In addition to engineering support, Mavex also serves as a resource for chemical system parts, tubing, piping, and specialty components.

Chemical Systems Design

Design support for industrial chemical systems with a focus on performance, safety, and constructability.

Drawings & Documentation

P&IDs, construction drawings, and fabrication drawings developed to support design, coordination, fabrication, and field execution.

Chemical Parts, Tubing & Piping

Support with pricing and sourcing chemical system parts, tubing, piping, and specialty components for industrial applications.

Specialty Component Solutions

Support for custom parts and specialty components, including solutions for terminating double-containment chemical systems while managing potential leaks and fumes to protect operators.

Skid Design & Build Assistance

Skid design support and assistance through the build process for specialty chemical systems.

Commissioning Support

Startup and commissioning assistance to help ensure systems perform as intended.
