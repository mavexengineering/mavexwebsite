Full-Lifecycle Engineering

What We Deliver

From concept to commissioning, Mavex delivers system design, documentation, components, and skid support for industrial applications.

Chemical Systems Design

Comprehensive engineering support for the design and integration of chemical process systems, including fluid handling, distribution networks, and process optimization. Solutions are tailored to meet safety standards, regulatory requirements, and operational efficiency goals in industrial and laboratory environments.

  • Process flow diagrams (PFDs)
  • Piping & instrumentation diagrams (P&IDs)
  • Chemical compatibility analysis
  • Process safety considerations (HAZOP)
  • Flow control & instrumentation selection

Drawings & Documentation

Development of detailed engineering drawings and technical documentation to support design, fabrication, and installation. Ensures clear communication between stakeholders and compliance with applicable standards and project requirements.

  • P&ID development & review
  • Isometric piping drawings
  • General arrangement (GA) drawings
  • Bill of materials (BOM)
  • Revision control & document management

Chemical Parts, Tubing & Piping

Support in the selection, specification, and sourcing of tubing, piping, valves, and fittings suitable for chemical service. Focus on material compatibility, pressure ratings, and long-term reliability in corrosive or high-purity environments.

  • Material compatibility (PVDF, PTFE, SS)
  • Tubing & fitting specification
  • Valve selection & sizing
  • Pressure rating & standards compliance
  • Supplier sourcing & technical evaluation

Specialty Component Solutions

Engineering support for custom and specialty components used in chemical systems, including unique terminations, transitions, and integration of proprietary equipment. Designed to meet specific application constraints and performance requirements.

  • Custom fitting & manifold design
  • Transition interfaces (metal–polymer)
  • High-purity component selection
  • Leak-tight connection solutions
  • Vendor-specific component integration

Double-Containment System Solutions

Design and implementation support for double-containment piping systems to ensure safe handling of hazardous chemicals. Focus on leak detection, secondary containment, and fume management to protect personnel and the environment.

  • Double-containment piping design
  • Leak detection systems
  • Secondary containment strategies
  • Fume exhaust integration
  • Regulatory compliance (SEMI, FM, local codes)

Skid Design & Build Assistance

Support for modular skid-based chemical systems, from initial layout and design through fabrication coordination and assembly. Ensures compact, transportable, and fully integrated system solutions.

  • 3D skid layout & integration
  • Equipment mounting & routing
  • Utility interface design
  • Fabrication & assembly coordination
  • Factory acceptance testing (FAT) support

Commissioning Support

Field support during system startup and commissioning to verify installation quality and operational performance. Includes troubleshooting, validation, and documentation to ensure systems meet design intent.

  • Startup & commissioning procedures
  • System validation & testing
  • Troubleshooting & issue resolution
  • Operator training support
  • As-built documentation updates
